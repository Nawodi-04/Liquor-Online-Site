St Remy XO

St Remy XO is a rich and complex brandy that remains true to the style of French brandy making. Luminous amber colour in appearance. The brandy has aromas of oak and vanilla cleverly layered with hints of fruits and honey. On the palate, flavours of gingerbread, apricots, figs, and nuts are enriched with subtle notes of wood and vanilla with a long finish. Best enjoyed neat, in ice, or in cocktails.
