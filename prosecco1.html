Bottega Prosecco Spumante

The Bottega Prosecco Spumante DOCG is a brut sparkling wine made with 100% Glera grapes by the traditional method. Beautiful straw yellowish gold in colour, the process has fine and consistent perlage. On the nose, fruity aromas of citrus fruits, white peach and apple, blend in nicely with fine floral notes of wisteria and acacia. On the palate, the wine is fresh, balanced, and delicate with a blend of softness and acidity. Best served on its own, or in cocktails, or as an aperitif. Best served with Italian dishes such as pizza, pasta, risotto and is an excellent complement to fish and meat courses too.
