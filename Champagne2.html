Taittinger Brut Reserve

Combining Chardonnay (40%), Pinot Meunier and Pinot Noir (60%) from over 35 vineyards, the Taittinger Brut Reserve is matured to perfection. Unique among fine non-vintage champagnes, the Taittinger Brut Reserve has a high proportion of Chardonnay grapes and is aged for a minimum of three years in cellars. This helps the champagne reach aromatic maturity, and a delicately blanced champagne.
