Crystal Head Vodka

The Crystal Head Vodka is a result of four distillations of locally sourced Canadian corn. This is then filtered seven times including three filterations through layers of semi precious Herkimer diamond crystals.

The skull shaped bottle is made of melted crystal ensuring absolute clarity. The vodka is clear, clean, and colourless with neutral aromas with a dash of citrus. On the palate, the vodka has hints of sweetness and vanilla and ends with a sweet, creamy, finish.
